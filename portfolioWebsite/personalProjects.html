  • Chronicles of the Forgotten Isles

    Chronicles of the Forgotten Isles is planned as an open-world MMORPG adventure game where players explore a chain of mysterious islands, each filled with ancient ruins, hidden treasures, and dangerous creatures. The game will focus on exploration, environmental puzzles, and uncovering the history of the forgotten civilization that once inhabited the islands.

    • Planned Features:
      A vast archipelago to explore, with each island offering unique biomes, puzzles, and secrets.
    • An emphasis on non-linear exploration, allowing players to discover the story at their own pace through environmental storytelling and artifact collection.
    • A crafting system that lets players create tools and gear from resources found on the islands, enhancing their survival and exploration abilities.

  • Astral Convergence

    Inspired by my love of astronomy and stargazing, Astral Convergence is envisioned as a single-player action RPG set in a cosmic fantasy universe where players harness the powers of the stars to battle celestial beings. The game will feature a deep skill tree system that allows players to align with different constellations, unlocking unique abilities and combat styles based on their chosen star path.

    • Planned Features:
      A vast, open-world environment with diverse celestial realms, each with its own unique challenges and lore.
    • A character progression system tied to constellations, where player choices shape their abilities and story.
    • Dynamic combat mechanics that blend fast-paced action with strategic use of star-based powers.
