Skills & References

About Me

My name is C. Elliott Lawson, a game developer and designer with a unique blend of experiences that have shaped my creative approach over the past decade. Before stepping into the world of game development, I spent 10 years as a mixologist and bar manager, where I mastered the art of crafting memorable experiences. This background not only honed my attention to detail and passion for storytelling but also pushed the boundaries of my creativity, enabling me to think outside the box and approach game design with a fresh perspective.

Knowledge & Skills

  • Storytelling & narrative development: Expertise in crafting immersive narratives and character-driven plots, including branching storylines, environmental storytelling, and dialogue writing.
  • Character & level design: Blender, Maya
  • Technical proficiency with multiple coding languages and game devlopment softwares: C++, C#, Python, JavaScript, Unity, Unreal Engine
  • Game Design and Mechanics: Proficient in designing engaging gameplay systems, from core mechanics to complex level designs, ensuring a compelling and balanced player experience.

Qualifications

  • BSc in Computer Science from the University of London
  • Certified Interactive Media Designer (CIMD)
  • Strategic Gameplay Analyst (SGA)
  • Diploma in Interactive Art & Design (DIAD)
