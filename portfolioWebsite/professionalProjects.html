Professional Projects

Recent Projects

This section is dedicated to showcasing my most recent completed professional collaborative projects. Here, you'll find a collection of the work I've done in partnership with other talented professionals and businesses, each project highlighting our combined efforts and shared vision. These collaborations have allowed me to refine my skills, contribute to dynamic team environments, and bring complex, multifaceted projects to fruition. From game design and development to storytelling and character creation, I am grateful to have been a part of such talented teams.

  • Echoes of the Past

    Echoes of the Past is an action-adventure game set in a mythological world inspired by ancient civilizations. Players take on the role of a hero chosen by the gods to restore balance to the world by solving puzzles, battling mythical creatures, and uncovering hidden secrets. The game features a rich narrative, intricate level design, and a dynamic combat system.

    Key Contributions

    • Lead Game Designer - Designed and developed the core gameplay mechanics, including combat, puzzle-solving, and exploration.
    • Led a team of artists and writers to create a visually stunning and narratively compelling experience.
    • Implemented an AI system for enemy behavior, enhancing the challenge and immersion of the game.

  • Cyber Nexus

    Cyber Nexus is a strategy game set in a futuristic city where players control a network of hackers working to take down corrupt mega-corporations. The game combines real-time strategy with resource management, requiring players to carefully plan their moves, gather intel, and execute cyber-attacks to achieve their objectives.

    Key Contributions

    • Developer - Assisted to develop the game's core AI and strategy mechanics, creating a challenging and engaging experience for players.
    • Worked closely with UI/UX designers to create an intuitive and futuristic interface that aligns with the game's theme.
    • Implemented a branching narrative system that allows for multiple endings based on player decisions.

  • Solaris Odyssey

    Solaris Odyssey is a space exploration RPG that allows players to captain their own spaceship, explore distant galaxies, and engage in tactical space combat. The game features a procedurally generated universe, offering endless exploration opportunities and unique encounters.

    Key Contributions

    • Lead World Builder - Designed the procedural generation system, creating a vast and varied universe for players to explore.
    • Led the development of the space combat mechanics, ensuring a balance between strategy and action.
    • Collaborated with sound designers and composers to create an immersive audio experience that enhances the game's atmosphere.

Older Projects

  • Eclipse

    Eclipse is an interactive visual novel that blends romance, mystery, and supernatural elements. Set in a small town where nothing is as it seems, players must uncover the truth behind a series of strange events while navigating complex relationships. The story is driven by character interactions, with multiple story paths and endings based on player decisions.

    Key Contributions

    • Lead Story Writer - Developed the primary narrative arc integrating romance, mystery, and supernatural themes into a cohesive story.
    • Wrote in-depth character dialogues and interactions, ensuring that each choice led to meaningful consequences.
    • Designed multiple narrative branches, giving players the freedom to explore different storylines and character relationships.

  • Guardians of the Elements

    Guardians of the Elements is a strategy-based RPG where players command a team of elemental warriors, each embodying a different natural force. Set in a world divided into elemental realms, players must strategically deploy their characters in battles that take advantage of the environment’s elemental properties.

    Key Contributions

    • Level Designer - Designed a variety of elemental-themed levels, each with unique terrain and environmental hazards that influence combat strategies.
    • Developed synergies between characters and environments, allowing players to exploit elemental advantages in battles and puzzles.

  • Pull of the Abyss

    Pull of the Abyss is a psychological horror game that delves into the human psyche. Players take on the role of a detective investigating a series of mysterious disappearances in a small coastal town. The story is filled with eerie twists, supernatural elements, and a deep exploration of the protagonist’s fears and inner demons.

    Key Contributions

    • Story Developer - Wrote immersive environmental storytelling elements, including journals, letters, and audio logs, to deepen the lore.
    • Assisted in constructing complex character arcs, focusing on the psychological depth and emotional impact of the story.

  • Neon Skies

    Neon Skies is a cyberpunk platformer set in a sprawling futuristic city. Players navigate through towering skyscrapers, neon-lit alleyways, and underground tech labs as a skilled hacker on the run. The game features a mix of fast-paced platforming and puzzle-solving, with an emphasis on verticality and exploration.

    Key Contributions

    • Level & Character Developer - Assisted in designing intricate levels that utilize vertical space, with multiple routes and hidden areas, enhancing replayability.
    • Assisted in the development of protaganist and NPC character designs focusing on the cyberpunk aesthetic.
